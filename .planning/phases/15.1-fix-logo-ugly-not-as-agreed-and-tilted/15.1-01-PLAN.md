**The correct α mark geometry (from the reference JPEG):**

The mark is drawn within a bounding box that is wider than tall (approx 1.3:1 width-to-height ratio for the full mark including tail). The approach:

1. **Draw the O-body as a thick ring (annulus):**
   - Outer circle: Large, nearly full circle centered at approximately (0.38*w, 0.48*h) with radius ~0.36*w
   - The circle has a GAP on the right side (between roughly -30° and +30° from horizontal)
   - Inner circle (counter/eye): Vertically elongated ellipse at same center, rx ~0.13*w, ry ~0.22*h
   - Use two arcs (outer and inner) combined to create the thick ring shape

2. **Draw the X-tail:**
   - UPPER stroke: Starts at the top of the O-body gap (~0.68*w, 0.22*h), curves upward-right to (~0.92*w, 0.05*h)
   - LOWER stroke: Starts at the bottom of the O-body gap (~0.68*w, 0.72*h), curves downward-right to (~0.92*w, 0.95*h)
   - Both strokes have the same thickness as the O-body ring (~0.12*w stroke width)
   - The strokes meet/overlap at the gap point (~0.72*w, 0.48*h)

**Implementation approach — use STROKE-based drawing for cleaner geometry:**

Instead of complex filled bezier paths, use `PaintingStyle.stroke` with a thick `strokeWidth` and `StrokeCap.round`:

```dart
@override
void paint(Canvas canvas, Size size) {
  final w = size.width;
  final h = size.height;
  
  final paint = Paint()
    ..color = color
    ..style = PaintingStyle.stroke
    ..strokeWidth = w * 0.13
    ..strokeCap = StrokeCap.round
    ..strokeJoin = StrokeJoin.round;

  // 1. The O-body: a large arc (nearly full circle) with a gap on the right
  // Center the O-body slightly left to leave room for the tail
  final oCenter = Offset(w * 0.38, h * 0.50);
  final oRadius = w * 0.30;
  
  // Draw arc from ~40° to ~320° (leaving gap on right from -40° to +40°)
  final oRect = Rect.fromCircle(center: oCenter, radius: oRadius);
  final oPath = Path();
  // Arc from 40° to 320° (280° sweep), angles in radians
  // Start at bottom of gap (40° = 0.698 rad), sweep counter-clockwise 280° (4.887 rad)
  oPath.addArc(oRect, 0.52, 5.24); // ~30° start, 300° sweep (leaves 60° gap on right)
  canvas.drawPath(oPath, paint);
  
  // 2. Upper tail stroke: from top-right of O gap, curving up-right
  final upperTail = Path();
  upperTail.moveTo(w * 0.60, h * 0.25);
  upperTail.cubicTo(
    w * 0.72, h * 0.18,
    w * 0.82, h * 0.10,
    w * 0.92, h * 0.05,
  );
  canvas.drawPath(upperTail, paint);
  
  // 3. Lower tail stroke: from bottom-right of O gap, curving down-right
  final lowerTail = Path();
  lowerTail.moveTo(w * 0.60, h * 0.75);
  lowerTail.cubicTo(
    w * 0.72, h * 0.82,
    w * 0.82, h * 0.90,
    w * 0.92, h * 0.97,
  );
  canvas.drawPath(lowerTail, paint);
}
```
